Information

Rules Of Play

Memory, also commonly known as Concentration, is a classic memory-based card game in which all the cards in the deck are laid face down, and then two cards are flipped face up. The object of the game is to turn over matching pairs of cards.

Deck Of Cards

Total 24-card deck containing 12 unique pairs of cards depicting various Star Wars characters.

Time Limit

Maximum 4 minutes.

Card Turn Over Limit

Maximum 108 flips (turn overs).

Goal

To match all pairs of cards in the least amount of time and with the least amount of flips.

Setup

Cards are randomnly shuffled and laid out in a grid face down.

Gameplay

Click 'Start' to begin play.

  • Click on the first card to turn face up and display the Star Wars character, and then click on a second card.
  • If the two cards display matching images, both cards remain face-up in position. Click to display another card.
  • If the two displayed cards do not match, both cards are turned back face down. Click to display another card.
  • Play continues until all the cards have been matched within the maximum time limit and maximum turn over (flips) limit.

Hint: Pay attention and remember where cards are located when flipped. When you turn over the first card, you may remember where to find the matching card.

Winning

All cards are matched in the least amount of time and the least number of cards flipped.

Game Over

Play will immediately stop if:

  • All cards are matched.
  • The maximum time is exceeded.
  • The maximum number of flips is exceeded.

Options

During game play, the following options are available:

  • Pause: click to temporarily pause the game.
  • Restart: click to refresh game board and start a new game.
